Tiramisu Recipe

What is Tiramisu?

Tiramisu is a popular Italian dessert made with layers of ladyfinger pastries dipped in coffee and whipped cream. It does not require many ingredients and can be prepared in a day, making it an easy recipe for beginners. To learn more about the history of tiramisu, visit this website!

Ingredients

• Around 30 ladyfingers

• 500 mg marcaspone cheese

• 4 medium eggs

• 1/2 cup granulated sugar

• 300 ml coffee

• Optional cocoa powder

• Around 30 ladyfingers

Instructions

Step 1: Make coffee with an espresso machine and set aside to cool.

Step 2: Separate the egg whites from yolks. Whip the egg whites at a high speed until stiff peaks form. Set aside.

Step 3: Combine the egg yolks with sugar and whip until smooth.

Step 4: Add the marscapone into the sugar and egg yolks. Slowly whip until fully combined. Now add the stiff egg whites and fold in with a wooden spoon.

Step 5: Dip ladyfingers into coffee for 1-2 seconds. Note: Be careful not to soak for too long, or else they will become soggy. Place ladyfingers on the bottom of a container of your liking.

Step 6: Once you have covered the bottom, add a layer of the whipped cream mixture. Continue this process with the rest of the ladyfingers.

Step 7: Once you have finished, generously sprinkle the top with cocoa powder.

NOTE: Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving. If possible, letting it rest overnight is optimal.
