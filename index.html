Tiramisu Recipe

What is Tiramisu?

Tiramisu is a popular Italian dessert made with layers of ladyfinger pastries dipped in coffee and whipped cream. It does not require many ingredients and can be prepared in a day, making it an easy recipe for beginners. To learn more about the history of tiramisu, visit this website!

Ingredients

  • Around 30 ladyfingers
  • 500 g marscapone cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • Around 30 ladyfingers
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 300 ml coffee
  • optional cocoa powder

Instructions

Step 1: Make coffee with an espresso machine and set aside to cool.

Step 2: Separate the egg whites from yolks. Whip the egg whites at a high speed until stiff peaks form. Set aside.

Step 3: Combine the egg yolks with sugar and whip until smooth.

Step 4: Add the marscapone into the sugar and egg yolks. Slowly whip until fully combined. Now add the stiff egg whites and fold in.
